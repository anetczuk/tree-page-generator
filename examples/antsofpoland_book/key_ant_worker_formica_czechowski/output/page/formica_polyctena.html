Formica polyctena
  • 1: Occipital margin of head straight, convex or, at most, slightly concave (Plates XV: 11, 12; XVI: 1-4)
  • 5: Bicoloured species, with alitrunk red and contrasting with brownish black gaster; alitrunk often with darker patches
  • 9: Anterior clypeal margin convex, not notched medially (Plate XVI: 1-4)
  • 10: Frontal triangle shiny, contrasting with dull surface of other parts of head (subg. Formica s.str.)
  • 15: At least upper third of head dorsum brownish black
  • 16: Occipital margin of head without or only with not abundant short erect to suberect hairs (Plate XVI: 3, 4). Alitrunk dorsum without or, at most, with a few standing hairs (Plate XVI: 7, 8)
  • 18: Occipital margin of head without hairs (Plate XVI: 4)
  • 19: Each segment of alitrunk dorsum without or with less than 6 standing hairs (Plate XVI: 8); ventral surface of head without or, at most, with short sparse suberect hairs (to be viewed in profile)
