Lasius subumbratus:
Info: https://antwiki.org/wiki/Lasius_subumbratus
  • 1: Metapleural gland opening provided with conspicuous guard hairs; in side view the scutum does not overhang the pronotum but shares with it the anterior thoracic convexity
  • 2: HW about the same as or greater than the width of the thorax just anterior to the regulae
  • 13: HW exceeding 1.00 mm.; petiole in side view thinner, with a narrow dorsal crest
  • 14: Pilosity of alitrunk and gaster otherwise; HW never exceeding 1.75 mm
  • 15: Dorsal border of the petiole with never more than a right-angular emargination
  • 16: HW at least 1.40 mm
  • 18: The longest hairs of the first gastric tergite, exclusive of the extreme posterior strip, about as long as the maximum width of the hind tibia at its midlength
  • 21: Pilosity of anterior gastric tergites predominantly decumbent-subdecumbent
