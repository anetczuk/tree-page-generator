Characteristic 15:
The dorsal border of the petiole in frontal view narrow and deeply emarginate, the depth of the emargination measured from the level of the bicornuate dorsal crest to the bottom of the emargination at least as great as the width of the emargination measured at the level of the middle of the depth measurement (Eurasia) Dorsal border of the petiole with never more than a right-angular emargination
