Lasius crispus:
Info: https://antwiki.org/wiki/Lasius_crispus
  • 1: Metapleural gland opening lacking guard hairs; in side view the scutum overhangs the pronotum and claims all of the anterior thoracic convexity
  • 22: An “alpha” form, showing no conspicuous flattening of the appendages; thorax with abundant hairs
  • 24: Many of the body and appendage hairs curved at the tip or sinuate, those on the appendages often curving back to touch the cuticular surface again; body pubescence sparse or absent, the cuticular surface smooth and shining
