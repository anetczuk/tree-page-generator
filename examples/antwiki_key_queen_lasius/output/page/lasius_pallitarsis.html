Lasius pallitarsis:
Info: https://antwiki.org/wiki/Lasius_pallitarsis
  • 1: Metapleural gland opening provided with conspicuous guard hairs; in side view the scutum does not overhang the pronotum but shares with it the anterior thoracic convexity
  • 2: HW distinctly less than the width of the thorax just anterior to the regulae
  • 3: Length of terminal segment of maxillary palp exeeeding 0.1 X the HW, even if only slightly
  • 4: When viewed with mandibles opened and the head held in perfect full face (at maximum head length and with the occipital and anterior clypeal borders horizontally aligned), the median third of the anterior border of the median clypeal lobe is flat or emarginate; all the queens of a nest series with at least one and often two or three offset teeth present on the basal angle and along the basal border of the mandible (North America)
