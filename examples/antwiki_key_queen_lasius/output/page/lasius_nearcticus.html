Lasius nearcticus:
Info: https://antwiki.org/wiki/Lasius_nearcticus
  • 1: Metapleural gland opening provided with conspicuous guard hairs; in side view the scutum does not overhang the pronotum but shares with it the anterior thoracic convexity
  • 2: HW distinctly less than the width of the thorax just anterior to the regulae
  • 3: Length of terminal segment of maxillary palp less, than 0.1 X the HW, even if by only a slight amount
  • 10: Scape with few or no standing hairs
  • 11: Tibiae with few or no standing hairs
  • 12: Terminal segment of maxillary palp usually longer than the penultimate (see diagnosis) (eastern North America to Wyoming)
